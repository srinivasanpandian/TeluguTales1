Grandmother's Tales

In the warm embrace of my grandmother's stories, Telugu folklore comes alive. Each evening, as the sun sets and the stars begin to twinkle, her gentle voice weaves magic through ancient tales of wisdom, love, and tradition. From the mischievous Tenali Raman to the brave stories of our ancestors, every word carries the essence of our rich cultural heritage.

Sankranti

The harvest festival celebrated with colorful rangoli, flying kites, and traditional sweets like Pongal and Ariselu.

Ugadi

A floral festival celebrating the goddess of life, with special rice preparations and sweets.

Legends of the Royal Kitchen

Royal Biryani

A fragrant rice dish with tender meat, aromatic spices, and caramelized onions, perfected in the royal kitchens of Hyderabad.

Royal Dessert

A decadent sweet treat made with milk, nuts, and aromatic spices, reserved for the most special occasions.

Royal Kebab

Succulent meat marinated in royal spices and grilled to perfection, a favorite of the Nizam's court.

Royal Pulao

An elegant rice dish with nuts, dried fruits, and subtle spices, served at royal banquets and special occasions.

Our Story

Preserving Telugu Culinary Heritage Since 2018

Welcome to Tales of Telugu, where traditional recipes meet heartfelt storytelling. Our journey began with a simple mission: to preserve the rich culinary heritage of Telugu cuisine through the stories of those who kept these traditions alive.

We take pride in documenting authentic recipes passed down through generations, capturing the essence of Telugu culture through its diverse flavors and cooking techniques.
